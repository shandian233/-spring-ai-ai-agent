Key Departments at Honor:

1. Product Development Department
   - Responsible for designing and developing smartphones, wearables, laptops, and other smart devices. Includes hardware engineering, software integration, and user experience design.

2. Research and Innovation (R&D) Department
   - Focuses on cutting-edge technologies such as camera systems, display innovations (e.g., OLED and high refresh rate screens), AI algorithms, and battery efficiency.

3. Marketing and Brand Management Department
   - Oversees global branding strategies, advertising campaigns, social media engagement, and product launches across different regions.

4. Sales and Distribution Department
   - Manages partnerships with retailers, distributors, and e-commerce platforms worldwide.

5. Customer Service and Support Department
   - Handles post-sales support, warranty services, technical assistance, and feedback collection.

6. Supply Chain and Operations Department
   - Ensures efficient procurement of components, manufacturing coordination, logistics, and inventory management globally.

7. Software and Services Department
   - Develops and maintains Honor’s software ecosystem, including Magic UI, cloud services, and app integrations.

8. Global Strategy and Business Development
   - Focuses on international expansion, market analysis, regulatory compliance, and strategic partnerships.
