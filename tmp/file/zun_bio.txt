ZUN (Jun'ya Ōta)
Born: March 18, 1977
Birthplace: Hakuba village, Nagano Prefecture, Japan

Background:
- Grew up playing video games from kindergarten (around 1982-1983)
- Enjoyed Game & Watch systems and arcade games
- Childhood home was a cafe with many arcade cabinets
- Also collected bugs as a child
- Rarely watched TV or read manga; focused on video games
- In middle school, loved Super Famicom and RPGs like Final Fantasy IV
- In high school, became obsessed with fighting games (Street Fighter 2, NEO-GEO)
- Did not study much for university entrance exams
- Was recommended to Tokyo Denki University through his high school

Game Development:
- Started learning programming during university
- Used PC-9821 computers for game development
- Learned programming entirely by self-study
- First games included a Puyo Puyo clone and shoot 'em ups using Dezaemon engine
- Creator of the Touhou Project series, which began in 1996
- As Team Shanghai Alice, he handles all aspects: programming, music, writing, and art

Personality:
- Known for drinking beer during interviews and streams
- Describes game development as "like playing a game" - finding joy in each small success
